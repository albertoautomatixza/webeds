Who we are

Engineering that moves industry forward

Engineering Design & Service is a Mexican engineering firm specializing in industrial automation, electrical projects, and Industry 4.0 solutions for the automotive sector. Over a decade of proven results.

Our story

Built on experience, driven by results

Founded with the vision of bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and real-world industrial demands, Engineering DS has grown into a trusted partner for some of the largest automotive manufacturers in Mexico.

From electrical control panels and PLC programming to full-scale automation lines, every project we deliver is backed by rigorous engineering standards, strict safety compliance, and a commitment to on-time execution.

Our team of certified engineers and technicians brings decades of combined experience — always seeking towards improvement.
