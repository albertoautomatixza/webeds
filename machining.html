Machining Processes

01

CNC Milling

3, 4, and 5-axis CNC milling for complex geometries, fixtures, molds, and structural components. We hold tight tolerances on aluminum, steel, stainless, titanium, and engineering plastics with consistent, repeatable results.

3–5 Axis Aluminum Steel Titanium
02

CNC Turning

High-precision CNC lathe turning for shafts, bushings, pulleys, and rotational components. Live tooling and sub-spindle capabilities allow complete part processing in a single setup, reducing lead times and handling errors.

Live Tooling Swiss-type OD / ID
03

Laser Cutting

Fiber and CO₂ laser cutting for sheet metal, tubing, and structural profiles with burr-free edges and minimal heat-affected zones. Ideal for high-volume flat patterns, brackets, enclosures, and custom fabrications.

Fiber Laser CO₂ Sheet Metal Tube Cutting
04

EDM Wire

Wire electrical discharge machining for hardened steels, exotic alloys, and intricate profiles where conventional cutting is not feasible. Achieves micron-level precision on dies, punches, gears, and fine-feature components.

Hardened Steel Exotic Alloys Dies & Punches
05

Welding

Certified MIG, TIG, and spot welding for structural assemblies, frames, and precision weldments. We work with carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum alloys, ensuring full penetration, dimensional stability, and weld quality per AWS/ISO standards.

MIG TIG Spot AWS / ISO
06

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Full-service sheet metal fabrication including shearing, bending, punching, forming, and finishing. From single prototypes to production runs, we deliver precision-formed enclosures, panels, brackets, and structural components ready for assembly.

Bending Punching Forming Finishing
